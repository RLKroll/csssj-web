 Elton Roberts - Electric Cactus Acid Test

We have learned from Elton Roberts regular feature in the Cactus Courier, "Grower's Garden". In particular, Elton has provided us with his analysis of water pH and how it affects the growth and habits of cacti and succulents. Our program for January will be a presentation by Elton on this subject.

Additionally, the theme for the Mini Show will be what we have learned from Elton's column. Members are encouraged to bring in plants that they have learned about in his articles, plants that have benefited from what they have garnered from his articles, or any plant that relates to his articles in any way.
