Newsletter of the Cactus and Succulent Society of San Jose

Cactus Grafting Workshop

Tony Bassano and Chuck Breese will be demonstrating their techniques for grafting cacti. Both members have excelled at grafting a variety of plants with great success.



Staging for Show

To make it easier for members to participate in our next Show, we are going to have demonstrations on staging plants for show in January, several months before our April Show. This will give members who want to show their plants a longer lead-time to complete their own staging and have their plants grow into their new homes.






Members with last names starting with M through Z should bring a snack for the January meeting.
