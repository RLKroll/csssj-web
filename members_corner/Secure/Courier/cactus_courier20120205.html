 Share What You Love!

Our next meeting will be an abbreviated one, so that we can all make home in time to watch the Super Bowl kick-off at 3:30. In order to facilitate that, there won’t be a formal mini show at the meeting; we will have a “show and tell” program instead.

Think about the following suggestions, and be prepared to discuss the plants that you bring to the meeting:

  What's your favorite cactus?
  What's your favorite succulent?
  Valentine theme (red/pink flowers, plants, spines, or blush)
  What's the plant that got you started with cacti or succulents?

We look forward to seeing what you’ll bring in!
