 Roger Lane - Bromeliads

Roger has been growing bromeliads since 1978, and has filled two greenhouses with them. He is a master bromeliad judge and has judged bromeliad shows all over the United States.

His program will consist of an overview of the bromeliad family supplemented with a slide show. All of the raffle plants will be bromeliads, but there will not be any bromeliads for sale.
