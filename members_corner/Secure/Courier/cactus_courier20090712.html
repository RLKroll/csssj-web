 Plant Rejuvenation

Naomi Bloss will be presenting a program on plant rejuvenation. She will be discoursing on propagation techniques, as well.

Bring in your tired, your poor, your yearning to be free of yellowing. Naomi will give you the benefit of her years of experience in saving ailing plants.

For those of you with plants beyond saving, there will be plants from her nursery available for sale.

Naomi is a long-time member of our club and has garnered frequent recognition for the quality, healthiness, and presentation of her entries at shows. As a professional grower, she has tremendous experience with rescuing plants in distress.
