 Plants Bought at the Sale

Every one of us cannot resist that particular plant calling to us from the sale tables at the Show and Sale. If we could, we would not be members of this club. This meeting is going to be all about sharing with your fellow members the allure that drew you to that special plant. Maybe you know precisely why that cactus or succulent called to you; maybe you do not have a clue. Sharing the experience with the rest of the club may just be the thing that will give you an insight into your own thought processes. In any case, bring that plant with you to the meeting, and we can all revel in our good fortune and exquisite taste.
