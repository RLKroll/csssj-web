 Impressions of South Africa: Succulents, Flowers, and Animals

Peter Beiersdorfer's presentation is based on three trips to South Africa exploring the Johannesburg - Kruger, Durban, and Capetown regions. The talk will highlight some of the sights anyone can see by being in the right place at the right time.
