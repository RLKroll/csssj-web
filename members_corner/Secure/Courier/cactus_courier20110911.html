 Peter Beiersdorfer - Growing Succulents from Seed

Peter Beiersdorfer will give a hands-on demonstration on how to grow cacti and other succulents from seed. He will discuss seed and soil preparation, germination, watering, dealing with pests, and re-potting. He will bring cactus and succulent seeds adapted for different growing conditions, so that everyone can try their hands in growing new plants. Much of the presentation will also be useful for keeping adult plants alive and healthy.

Peter will be bringing plants for sale.
