 Woody Minnich - Flora of Brazil

For this meeting, Woody will be focusing on cacti observed during his recent trip to Brazil.

Many members will remember several wonderful programs that Mr. Minnich has presented to our club over the years. Always a source of fascinating information about succulents and other types of plants, Woody also brings with him a variety of choice plants for sale.




Note that our meetings will not be held on the first Sunday of the month for July, August, September, and October due to a variety of conflicts.







We will be having a pot luck for the October meeting.
