 Trip to Ruth Bancroft Gardens

We will be meeting at the Ruth Bancroft Gardens at 10:00am on July 8th.

1500 Bancroft Road, Walnut Creek, CA, 94598
http://www.ruthbancroftgarden.org/index.html

From San Francisco/San Jose (South):
Take Highway 680 North and exit at the Ygnacio Valley Road exit. Turn right onto Ygnacio Valley Road and follow for 2 miles to Bancroft Road. Turn left onto Bancroft Road and The Garden is located on the right.

Barbecue at the Munkacsy's

The tour of the gardens will be followed by a barbecue at the home of Bill and Karla Munkacsy, 300 Miranda Lane, Alamo, CA, 94507 (925-935-5213). Plan to be there around 12:00 noon. Bring utensils, drinks, cups, and side dishes to share with each other.
