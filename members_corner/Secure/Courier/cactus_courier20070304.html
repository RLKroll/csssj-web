 Naomi Bloss - Trip to Argentina Continued

Now that Naomi and Frank Bloss have taken us through northern Argentina, they are going to move south toward Patagonia. Climates and vegetation are much different there. Native cacti include Opuntia, Echinopsis, Neoporteria, and Gymnocalicium.
