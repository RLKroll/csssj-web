Our next meeting is abbreviated, so we can all make it home in time for the Super Bowl kick-off at 3:30. To help make that happen, we won't have a formal Mini Show at the meeting, but we'll have a “show and tell” program instead.

Each CSSSJ member, though, can still earn Mini Show points for the plants you bring for show and tell. You can bring a maximum of 5 plants and earn 5 points for each plant, for a total of 25 Mini Show points. What a deal, and no voting!

Think about the following suggestions, and be prepared to discuss the plants that you bring to the meeting:

      What's your favorite cactus?
      What's your favorite succulent?
      Valentine theme (red/pink flowers, plants, spines, or blush)
      Echeveria

We look forward to seeing what you bring in!
