 Naomi Bloss - Potting Workshop

Several members have asked about help with preparing their plants for showing or just to make them more attractive for home display. This month, Naomi Bloss, one of our most experienced and successful growers and blue ribbon winners, will hold a hands-on demonstration of the techniques that have served her so well. She will touch on displaying, propagating, and rejuvenating succulent plants.

If you are interested to competing and winning in one of our renowned shows, or if you just want your plants to show their better side, this is a meeting you do not want to miss.

The Bloss family will bring plants for sale.
