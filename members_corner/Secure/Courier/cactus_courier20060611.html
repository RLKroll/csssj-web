Culture According to Naomi

Naomi Bloss, well known as a superior propagator and grower, will present a program covering a wide range of topics describing how she achieves her wonderful show and sale plants.

Most of us have many questions regarding propagating, revitalizing, and maintaining our favorite plants. Bring a plant and have Naomi discuss how to divide it, make cuttings, and care for the new material.

The Bloss family will be bringing plants for sale.









Members with last names starting with G through L should bring a snack for the June meeting.
