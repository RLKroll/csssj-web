Travels in Argentina - Mark Muradian

Mark Muradian's grandfather started farming in the San Joaquin Valley in 1915. Mark still carries on his family's tradition. He has been cultivating cacti and succulents for nearly 20 years. He is a frequent and popular presenter to our club.

This time, he is regaling us with a presentation on his recent trip to Argentina. He was accompanied on this excursion by Woody Minnich, Frank and Naomi Bloss, Roger Lane, Marilyn Moyer, Peder Samuelson, and many others.

He will bring a variety of his fine, handmade pots for sale just in time for you to be able to repot that prizewinning plant for the Show.
