 Ernesto Sandoval - Flora of the Southwest

Ernesto Sandoval is the curator at the Conservatory at U.C. Davis. As such, he has great experience in propagating plants (and keeping them alive.) He will be bringing plants from the Conservatory for sale. Mr. Sandoval has done extensive traveling in the Southwest and Mexico. The topic for this presentation will be the flora of the Southwest.
