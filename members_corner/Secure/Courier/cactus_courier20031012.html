 Mark Muradian - Desert Landscaping

Mark Muradian is a third-generation farmer in the San Joaquin valley. This affords him the benefit of large-scale farming equipment to do his landscaping. The result is desert tableaus on a grand scale. His slide show is well worth the watching, if for no other reason than to marvel at the magnitude of his efforts.

He is an accomplished potter and will be bringing prime examples of his art for sale.
