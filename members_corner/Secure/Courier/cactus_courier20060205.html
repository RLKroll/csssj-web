Preparation for Show and Sale

The February program will be a continuation of our preparation for this year's show.

Please bring a plant you want to prepare to enter in our annual show.

Wes Briggs will be providing hands-on help for members who want to dress up their favorite plants for the show or just want to see their plant at its best.

Bring that beautiful succulent plant that has been maturing for years in that old, cylindrical clay and a pot in which you would like to see it growing. (We will try to have show quality pots available for purchase at this meeting.) See it go home with you ready to win a blue ribbon.

Growing medium and top dressing will be provided.

Wes Briggs will be bringing plants for sale.








Members with last names starting with M through Z should bring a snack for the February meeting.
