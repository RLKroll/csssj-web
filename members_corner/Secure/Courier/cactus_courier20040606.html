Roger Lane - Argentina Trip

In the Southern Hemisphere summer of 2003, Roger Lane explored the flora of Argentina and Chile. He will be presenting a digital slide show of his finds in Argentina at our June meeting.

Mr. Lane is a renowned expert on succulent plants and Bromeliads and has been a show judge for both.














We are having a potluck for the June meeting.
