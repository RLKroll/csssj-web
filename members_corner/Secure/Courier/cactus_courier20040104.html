 Larry Grammer - Staging for Shows

Well known for his plant staging techniques in Southern California, Larry Grammer will be providing us with insights into his preparation methodology. Larry presented this program at NORCAL to rave reviews. This could be the last chance in a long time for us to learn about Mr. Grammer's staging techniques.

He will be bringing pots and plants for demonstration purposes. Next month, Naomi Bloss and Rowena Southwell will conduct hands-on exercises for aspiring stagers where you can apply the lessons learned in Mr. Grammer's presentation and demonstrations.








Munchies for the January meeting will be a potluck. Bring your favorite dish.
